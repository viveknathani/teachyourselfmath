I want to start a little math club in Bangalore.

Why

Since finishing college a few years ago, studying math has been a mostly solitary pursuit for me. While I enjoy exploring new concepts and solving problems on my own, I often miss the collaborative spirit of my academic days. Back then, my friends and I would gather to discuss homework problems, share insights, and learn from each other. There was a unique joy in exploring ideas together, something that’s hard to replicate alone.

I’d love to bring back that sense of community, where math isn’t just an isolated exercise but a shared experience.

If I could bring a bunch of math enthusiasts under the same roof for a few hours, here are a few ideas for what we could do:

  • We can work on a little problem set together.
  • We can work on our own problem sets.
  • We can do some form of competitive math, by deriving ideas from competitive programming.
  • We can talk about ideas from math that are interesting to us.
  • We can take a book club approach: agree upon a book that everybody reads, share our progress, discuss concepts, etc..

I’m sure more ideas will emerge once we get started. My goal is simply to take the first step and see where this goes.

How

Bangalore’s cafe scene offers a great setting for a small gathering. I’d prefer starting small, meeting at a cafe on a weekend. I’m thinking of hosting the first meetup on Saturday, 15th March 2025. The exact location is still TBD.
